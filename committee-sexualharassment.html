shieldPOSH Act, 2013 Compliance

This committee is constituted as per the Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013. All complaints are handled with strict confidentiality.

About

Objective & Purpose

The Sexual Harassment Prevention Committee (Internal Complaints Committee) ensures the prevention, prohibition, and redressal of sexual harassment at the workplace and educational institution. It creates a safe and respectful environment for everyone on campus.

Role

Activities & Functions

  • check_circleReceiving and investigating complaints of sexual harassment on campus.
  • check_circleOrganising awareness workshops on POSH Act, rights, and responsibilities.
  • check_circleMaintaining strict confidentiality of complainants and proceedings.
  • check_circleRecommending action as per the provisions of the POSH Act, 2013.
  • check_circleSubmitting annual reports to the management as required by law.
