Objective & Purpose

The Library Committee oversees the development of the college library, including book procurement, journal subscriptions, digital resource management, and ensuring equitable student access to all library facilities.

  • check_circleReviewing library timings and framing rules for student access.
  • check_circleSupervising digital library infrastructure and e-resource subscriptions.
  • check_circleMonitoring library usage patterns and addressing student feedback.
