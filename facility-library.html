About Our Library

Information is the backbone of the future. It lies at the core of research and serves as the foundation of learning. Ganga Mahavidyalaya ensures that both students and faculty have access to rich and diverse sources of knowledge.

Our well-stocked library houses a vast collection of nearly 6,000 books along with access to online resources. It offers materials across a wide range of subjects, supporting academic growth, research, and intellectual development.

Ganga Mahavidyalaya takes pride in having one of the most well-established libraries in the region. The library caters to students, teachers, and researchers, providing comprehensive resources across various branches of knowledge.

The facility is fully automated and equipped with modern infrastructure, including CCTV surveillance for security and fire-fighting systems for safety. The college also subscribes to a wide range of books, journals, and periodicals covering multiple disciplines.
