About

Objective & Purpose

The Student Grievance Redressal Committee provides a mechanism for students to voice their concerns and complaints. It ensures that all grievances are addressed promptly, impartially, and confidentially in accordance with UGC guidelines.

Process

How to File a Grievance

1
Submit Written Complaint

Submit a written grievance to the committee convener or drop it in the grievance box.

2
Acknowledgment within 3 Days

The committee will acknowledge receipt and assign a complaint number.

3
Resolution within 15 Days

Investigation and resolution will be completed within 15 working days.
