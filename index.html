About College

Ganga Mahavidyalaya imparts education in programmes such as B.A. Civil Services, BBA, B.Com, B.Sc. Hospitality Studies and M.Lib. under diverse faculties. The institution is committed to national unity and integrity and is paving its way to become an institute of excellence to help the poor, needy and underprivileged.

Established under Vishwa Bahuuddeshiya Sevabhavi Sanstha (VBSS) in 2020, the college is affiliated to Kavikulaguru Kalidas Sanskrit University, Ramtek and approved by the Government of Maharashtra. It is situated at Kumbhphal Village, Chh. Sambhajinagar.
