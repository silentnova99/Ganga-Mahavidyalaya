About Ganga Mahavidyalaya

Founded with a vision to bring world-class higher education to the rural heartlands, Ganga Mahavidyalaya stands as a beacon of progress in Kumbhphal Village, Chh. Sambhajinagar.

Established under the Vishwa Bahuuddeshiya Sevabhavi Sanstha (VBSS), the college is affiliated to Kavikulguru Kalidas Sanskrit Vishwavidyalaya (KKS University), Ramtek, Nagpur and approved by the Government of Maharashtra.
