About The Course

Bachelor of Commerce

Bachelor of Commerce is a 3-year undergraduate course that offers diverse career options in the fields of Finance, Banking and Accounting.

After completing a B.Com degree, students get job profiles such as Auditor, Banker, Consultant etc. The course provides a strong theoretical and practical foundation in commerce, taxation, and business law.
