About

Objective & Purpose

The Career Counselling & Placement Committee guides students to identify their aptitude and career interests, connects them with industry opportunities, and supports applications for higher education and competitive examinations.

Activities & Functions

  • check_circleOrganising career counselling sessions and aptitude testing for students.
  • check_circleFacilitating campus placement drives and industry connect programmes.
  • check_circleGuiding students for competitive examinations like UPSC, MPSC, Banking, etc.
  • check_circleProviding information on higher education opportunities, scholarships, and fellowships.
  • check_circleResume building, interview skills, and soft skills development workshops.
