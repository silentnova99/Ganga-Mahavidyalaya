About

Objective & Purpose

The National Service Scheme (NSS) at Ganga Mahavidyalaya aims to develop the personality and character of students through community service. The motto "Not Me But You" reflects the essence of democratic living and spirit of selfless service.

NSS volunteers carry out regular activities as well as special camping programmes to develop their personalities and help communities in rural areas through education, health, and social service.
