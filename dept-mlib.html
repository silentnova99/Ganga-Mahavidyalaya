About The Course

Masters in Library Science

Masters in Library Science (M.Lib) is a postgraduate programme that trains students in library management, information science, cataloguing, classification, and digital library systems.

With the growth of digital information infrastructure, trained library professionals are in high demand across universities, government institutions, and corporate knowledge centres.

Programme Goals

Objectives

  • check_circleTrain students in modern library management techniques and digital cataloguing.
  • check_circleDevelop expertise in information retrieval, classification, and knowledge organisation.
  • check_circlePrepare students for roles in academic, public, special, and digital libraries.
