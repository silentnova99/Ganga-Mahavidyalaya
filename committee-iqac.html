About

Objective & Purpose

The Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) was established to ensure continuous quality improvement in academic and administrative processes. It serves as a catalyst for promoting institutional excellence and quality consciousness.

IQAC channelises the efforts and measures of an institution towards academic excellence. It coordinates various quality-related activities, collects and analyses feedback, and prepares the Annual Quality Assurance Report (AQAR) for NAAC.

  • check_circleOrganization of workshops and seminars on quality-related themes.
  • check_circleDocumentation of various academic and administrative activities for institutional records.
  • check_circlePreparation of the Annual Quality Assurance Report (AQAR) for NAAC.
