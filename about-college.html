Institutional Heritage

Vishwa Bahuuddeshiya Sevabhavi Sanstha (VBSS), Chh. Sambhajinagar is a growing education institution established in 2002. Ganga Mahavidyalaya was established under this institution in 2020 with the aim of empowering poor and rural students.

The college is affiliated to Kavikulguru Kalidas Sanskrit Vishwavidyalaya (KKS University), Ramtek, Nagpur and is approved by the Government of Maharashtra. It is situated at Kumbhphal Village, Chh. Sambhajinagar.

The college runs on a self-financial basis. VBSS is constantly striving toward perfection and engages in the overall development of students through various programme activities.

Vision

"To culture the quality development of entrepreneurship, through dedicated staff and transparent management for bringing about a social change through affordable education for the upliftment of students in rural areas to make them self-reliant."

Mission

"To be an organization providing quality education to the masses to bring about a social change in the region."
