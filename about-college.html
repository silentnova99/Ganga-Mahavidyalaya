Our Story

Institutional Heritage

Vishwa Bahuuddeshiya Sevabhavi Sanstha (VBSS), Chh. Sambhajinagar is a new and growing education institution established in 2002. Ganga Mahavidyalaya was established under this institution in 2020 with the aim of poor and rural student empowerment.

The college is affiliated to Kavikulguru Kalidas Sanskrit Vishwavidyalaya (KKS University), Ramtek, Nagpur and is approved by the Government of Maharashtra. It is situated at Kumbhphal Village, Chh. Sambhajinagar.

The college runs on a self-financial basis. The VBSS Sanstha is emerging and constantly striving hard on the way of perfection. The institute is always engaged with the overall development of the student through various programme activities.
