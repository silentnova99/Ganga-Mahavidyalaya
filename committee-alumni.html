About

Objective & Purpose

The Alumni Association of Ganga Mahavidyalaya maintains a strong network of graduates, facilitates mentorship between alumni and current students, organises alumni meets, and builds a resource base for institutional development through alumni contributions.

Role

Activities & Functions

  • check_circleMaintaining an updated alumni database and contact directory.
  • check_circleOrganising annual alumni meets and reunion events.
  • check_circleFacilitating mentorship programmes where alumni guide current students.
  • check_circleInviting alumni as guest speakers for career guidance sessions.
  • check_circleCollecting alumni feedback for curriculum and institutional improvement.
