About the Seminar Hall

The Seminar Hall at Ganga Mahavidyalaya is a versatile venue equipped with modern audio-visual infrastructure. It serves as the primary venue for guest lectures, workshops, seminars, cultural events, and examinations.

The hall is designed to accommodate a large number of students comfortably while providing excellent acoustics and visibility for speakers and presenters.
