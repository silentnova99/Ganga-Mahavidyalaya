About The Course

BBA — Business Administration

BBA is a 3-year undergraduate programme that equips students with managerial and business skills. The course develops leadership, communication, and entrepreneurial abilities to prepare students for the business world.

Especially relevant for rural youth looking to build their own enterprises or join the growing corporate sector, BBA at Ganga Mahavidyalaya combines theoretical knowledge with practical exposure.
