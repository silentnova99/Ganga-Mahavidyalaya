About The Course

B.Sc. Hospitality Studies

B.Sc. Hospitality Studies is a 3-year undergraduate programme for students who are seeking a career in the hospitality and services sector.

The course has been designed to offer students the knowledge of management of food, beverage and accommodation along with general management. It encompasses subjects such as Food Production, Housekeeping, Food Safety and Nutrition, and Hotel Accountancy.
