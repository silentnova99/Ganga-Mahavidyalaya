About The Course

B.Sc. Hospitality Studies

B.Sc. Hospitality Studies is a 3-year undergraduate programme for students seeking a career in the hospitality and services sector.

The course covers management of food, beverage and accommodation along with general management — including Food Production, Housekeeping, Food Safety and Nutrition, and Hotel Accountancy.
