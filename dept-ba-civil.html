About The Course

B.A. Civil Services

B.A. Civil Services is a 3-year undergraduate programme that provides various Social Science courses. Civil service is one that attracts the attention of people with high ambitions.

According to statistics, students who pursue Arts in Social Sciences have performed better in all Civil Services exams. A course in Sociology, Political Science or Economics gives you more opportunities in both private as well as government sectors.
