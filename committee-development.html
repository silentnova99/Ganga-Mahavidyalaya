About

Objective & Purpose

The College Development Committee plans and monitors the infrastructural, academic, and financial development of the college. It works towards achieving the institution's long-term vision and ensures proper utilization of resources for student benefit.

Role

Activities & Functions

  • check_circlePlanning infrastructure development including classrooms, labs, and facilities.
  • check_circleReviewing and approving the college development fund budget.
  • check_circleMonitoring academic quality and suggesting curriculum improvements.
  • check_circleReviewing college development proposals and recommending grants.
  • check_circleEnsuring that college development aligns with UGC and NAAC guidelines.
