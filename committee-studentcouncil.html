About

Objective & Purpose

The Student Council serves as the official representative body of students. It bridges communication between students and administration, organises cultural and sports events, and ensures student welfare and participation in institutional decision-making.

Activities & Functions

  • check_circleOrganising cultural events, sports competitions, and annual fests.
  • check_circleCoordinating student welfare activities and community service drives.
  • check_circleParticipating in IQAC and college development committee meetings.
