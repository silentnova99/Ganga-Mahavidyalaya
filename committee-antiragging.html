Anti-Ragging Committee

Preventing ragging, handling complaints, and creating a safe campus environment for all students.

Home/Committees/Anti-Ragging
warningRagging is a punishable offence

As per the Hon'ble Supreme Court directives and UGC regulations, ragging in any form is strictly prohibited. Offenders are liable for severe disciplinary action including expulsion.

Anti-Ragging Helpline: 1800-180-5522 (Toll Free)

About

Objective & Purpose

The Anti-Ragging Committee works to prevent ragging in any form within the college premises, handle complaints effectively, and create awareness among students about the legal consequences of ragging.

Role

Activities & Functions

  • check_circleMonitoring campus for any ragging incidents and taking prompt action.
  • check_circleReceiving and investigating anti-ragging complaints from students.
  • check_circleOrganising awareness programmes and orientation sessions for new students.
  • check_circleDisplaying anti-ragging notices and affidavit requirements prominently on campus.
