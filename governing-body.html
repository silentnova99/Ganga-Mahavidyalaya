A Message

From the Director's Desk

"Education is the prime important weapon to change the life."

The Sanstha established the institution Ganga Mahavidyalaya at Kumbhphal for educationally backward and economically poor students, with the aim of imparting quality education in the field of basic education sector.

We vow to take care of the academic as well as co-curricular component. The prescribed curriculum is taught in such a manner that every student can get appropriate and ample knowledge to face the challenges of the new era.

The effective resources and the environment provided by the College facilitate every student better learning and development. As a director of this growing institution, I assure you that we would support and provide to make our students competent enough to carve their future and also to help them to build the nation.

Khandelwal R. K. Director, Vishwa Bahuuddeshiya Sevabhavi Sanstha (VBSS), Chh. Sambhajinagar
