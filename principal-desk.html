Profile

About the Principal

Mrs. Manisha Rakesh Katyayani is a dedicated and experienced educational leader serving as the Principal of Ganga Mahavidyalaya. With over 16 years of experience in teaching and college administration, she is committed to providing quality education and fostering the overall development of students.

Under her leadership, the college has achieved excellence in academics, discipline, co-curricular activities, and community engagement. She believes in creating a positive learning environment that encourages innovation, creativity, and moral values among students.

Her vision is to empower students with knowledge, skills, and character so that they become responsible citizens and successful professionals in the future.
